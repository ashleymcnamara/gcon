July 12-15, 2017 :: Denver, Colorado

Three Day Conference with optional Workshop Day

GopherCon 2017 will be in Denver, Colorado again in 2017. The conference starts on July 13th, with an optional workshop day on the 12th. Our final day the 15th is the "Community Day".

The Largest Go Conference in the World

GopherCon is the original Go conference.

Hosted each summer in Denver, Colorado, GopherCon started in 2014, with an amazing first year attendance of over 700 people. Since then, we've doubled in attendance. We expect to sell out early this year, with a maximum capacity of 1500 people. Don't wait for the last minute to buy your tickets. We'd hate for you to be sad.
